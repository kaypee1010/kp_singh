My research interests include:

- Intelligent Systems and IoT

- Cybersecurity and Secure Computing
- AI for Social Impact

I have published research in reputed journals and conferences including Springer and IEEE venues, with nearly **400 citations**, an **h-index of 7**, and multiple patents filed in AI-based systems.
